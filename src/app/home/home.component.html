WHO WE WORK WITH

We have the ability to work with all clients regardless of skill level or goals. That said, we do have a specialty of working with athletes who have goals pertaining to strength sports such as powerlifting and strongman. We do also have experience working with, both female and male, bodybuilders in contest prep and off season growth.

HOW TO GET STARTED

Because our services are tailored to the individual, we offer a free (virtual) consultation where your future coach will:

  • Review your self-improvement goals, assets, and obstacles
  • Start outlining what form your individual improvement journey will take
  • Determine the commitment level needed to reach your goals*
  • Discuss availability to minimize session conflicts and schedule first full session



* We offer per session payment as well as a discount for making the commitment to at least one month(4 sessions) of self-improvement.
